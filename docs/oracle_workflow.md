The new combined divination, which we can call the **Tripartite Divination Workflow**, follows the formula for the **Combination Operator ($\oplus$):**
[cite_start]$$\mathbf{H}_{\text{Divination Workflow}} = \mathbf{H}_{\text{H}} \cup \mathbf{H}_{\text{T}} \cup \mathbf{H}_{\text{C}}$$ [cite: 124]

This workflow breaks the process down into three distinct, complementary phases: **Contextual Diagnosis (Horoscope), Active Assessment (Tarot),** and **Ethical Transition (I Ching).**

### Phase 1: Contextual Diagnosis (Horoscope)

[cite_start]This phase establishes the emotional and psychological state of the querent using the generalist and validating nature of the Horoscope[cite: 99].

* [cite_start]**Heuristic Focus:** **H4: The Validation Imperative** [cite: 99] [cite_start]and **H6: Contextual Priming**[cite: 104].
* **The Workflow Step:** The reading begins with a **Horoscope** statement for the day/week.
* [cite_start]**Action:** The Horoscope provides a **linguistically structured focus point** [cite: 88][cite_start], using the **Barnum Adjective Set (H2)** [cite: 93] [cite_start]to validate the querent's current mood and self-perception[cite: 99, 93]. [cite_start]It frames the current *internal tension* (e.g., "You are driven but may be feeling insecure about a recent decision")[cite: 90].
* [cite_start]**Output:** A generalized, low-risk **Validation of the emotional status quo** and an **Immediate-Near Future Window (H7)** focus[cite: 107].
    * [cite_start]**Goal Met:** Exploits the **Barnum Effect** [cite: 3] [cite_start]to establish a trusting, resonant foundation for the rest of the reading[cite: 72].

### Phase 2: Active Assessment (Tarot)

[cite_start]The validated emotional state is now mapped onto a specific, actionable narrative, moving from general emotion to concrete situation[cite: 67].

* [cite_start]**Heuristic Focus:** **T4: Situational Mapping** [cite: 67] [cite_start]and **T7: Foreshadowing, Not Fate**[cite: 75].
* **The Workflow Step:** A **Tarot Spread** (e.g., a three-card Past-Present-Future) is drawn *based on the internal tension identified in Phase 1*.
* [cite_start]**Action:** The cards are interpreted within the spread positions (T4) [cite: 67] [cite_start]using **Archetypal Symbolism (Tarot's Major/Minor Arcana)**[cite: 59, 61]. [cite_start]The reader **synthesizes the narrative (T5)** [cite: 70] [cite_start]to clarify the external situation, its root, and the *consequences* of the querent's present-day choices[cite: 75]. [cite_start]The **Reversibility Axiom (T3)** introduces the nuance of blocked or negative action[cite: 63].
* [cite_start]**Output:** A cohesive, active **narrative of the *transition* phase** [cite: 70] [cite_start]with a warning/possibility that is **dependent on action** (T7)[cite: 75].
    * [cite_start]**Goal Met:** Provides **Agency** [cite: 76] [cite_start]and breaks down the problem into elemental components (T2)[cite: 62].

### Phase 3: Ethical Transition (I Ching)

This phase moves from "What is happening?" to "What is the most harmonious action?" [cite_start]The I Ching offers the final, ethically grounded path forward[cite: 44].

* [cite_start]**Heuristic Focus:** **C4: The Primary and Secondary State** [cite: 35] [cite_start]and **C7: Alignment with Natural Law**[cite: 43].
* **The Workflow Step:** The question focuses specifically on the *action* required to navigate the transition identified in Phase 2. The **I Ching Hexagram** is cast.
* [cite_start]**Action:** The reading generates a **Present Hexagram** and a **Future Hexagram (C4)** [cite: 35] [cite_start]based on the **Dynamic Pivot (C3)**[cite: 31]. [cite_start]Interpretation focuses on the **Interplay of Tensions (C6)** [cite: 39] [cite_start]between the two states to find the path that aligns with **Natural Law (C7)**[cite: 44]. [cite_start]Crucially, the advice may be **Active, Timely Non-Intervention (C8)**[cite: 46].
* [cite_start]**Output:** Prescriptive guidance on the **required balance and ethical path** [cite: 40, 43][cite_start], focusing on *timing* and *non-action* as much as action[cite: 47].
    * [cite_start]**Goal Met:** Provides a sense of **Cosmic Order** [cite: 7] [cite_start]and ensures the advice is not *Self-Serving*[cite: 45].
